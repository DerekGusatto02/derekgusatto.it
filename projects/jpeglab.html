JPEGLab

  • UniPD
  • In Progress

Updated to 21st May 2025

JPEGLab is a web application for the study of JPEG encoding created as a project for the internal internship at the University of Padua - Department of Mathematics for the bachelor's thesis in Computer Science. The purpose of this internship project is the design and development of an application for educational purposes that allows to visualize and understand the JPEG encodings underlying images and the compression techniques. The objective is to provide an interactive and intuitive tool, aimed at students, to support the learning of fundamental concepts related to the digital representation of images. The student will have the task of analyzing the functional and educational requirements of the project, selecting the most suitable technologies, and creating an interactive interface that allows the user to upload images, explore their encoding and visualize the digital transformations in real time. Furthermore, he/she will have to take care of the technical documentation of the application.
